Pancakes

Description

Fluffy pancakes served with maple syrup and butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Add milk and eggs to the dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a griddle and pour batter to make pancakes.
