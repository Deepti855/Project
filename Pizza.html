Pizza

Description

A delicious homemade pizza with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and spread the pizza sauce over it.
  3. Add your favorite toppings and sprinkle with cheese.
