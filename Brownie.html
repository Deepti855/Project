Brownies

Description

Rich and chocolatey brownies that are perfect for dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Melt butter and mix with sugar and cocoa powder.
  2. Add eggs and vanilla extract, then mix in flour and salt.
  3. Pour batter into a baking dish and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 25-30 minutes.
